I'm Amaru Boyd

I’m a passionate gamer, but more importantly I’m passionate about game design. I’m an avid gamer that found the love for the art of games while playing them at an early age. This passion for games has caused me to pursue a degree in game design and create goals of being a part of great teams making great projects.

"Imaginary Shift"
Lead Programmer

Imaginary Shift is a futuristic racing game. The lead programmer role gave me responsability of creating scripts that ranged from creating the main menu and pause menu to creating the speed boost.

"The X'Tal"
Mechanics Designer

The X'Tal is a custom Warhammer: 40,000 army. I was a part of the team in charge of developing mechanics and designs for: units, stratagems, and weapons for our custom Warhammer army.

"Storm the Castle"
Level Designer

Storm the Castle is my first VR (Virtual Reality) project and the first time I had a major role in level design. While part of the level design team, I had to do level concept, prop placing, and quality assurance testing. We came across frame rate issues so we used our limited resources to give the dungeon feel.

Game Designer
