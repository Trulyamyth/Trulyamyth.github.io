I'm Amaru Boyd

I’m a game designer with an interest in UI/UX design. I enjoy being a part of the process of finding that fun factor in a game. Designing how a game plays and explaining why it works or how it works is what I enjoy most about game design.

Follow That Pipe

Game Designer

Gameplay Design

Follow that Pipe is a Discovery World game that teaches the player about energy. During this project, I was responsible for designing puzzles and playtesting those designs to make sure they wouldn’t be too difficult for kids to solve. This was done through a series of playtesting and iterating on feedback.
